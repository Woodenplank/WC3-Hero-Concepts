# --- Shade Binder ---


### Abilities
* Shadow Gate - Spawns a dark pit at target location which spews forth shadowy projectiles that strike nearby enemies.
* Unlight Shield (Cloak of Unlight) - some kind of shield spell... Unit target?
* Null Zone - reduces damage taken from projectiles, perhaps also periodic healing or draining from enemies - aura?
* Shadow Weave - launches a spiral of shadows towards target location, dealing damage to enemies along the way. The spiral then returns to the Hero, healing allies.
    Healing is increased based on number of enemies hit along the first arc.
* Projection - Spawns a shadowy amalgamation at target location, which will mimic the Hero's abilities (all of them??)
